Introduction

As most people know, this scenic place blessed with it's natural beauty, is home to world famous Darjeeling tea and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway- A world heritage. Darjeeling also has the largest orchid species, for example Cypripedium, Dendrobidium and so on. Although Darjeeling is a small city, it has the third tallest mountain in the world,Mount Kanchenjunga. It also has Tiger Hill,so tall that you could get a glimpse over to Mount Everest.
