Introduction

As most people know, this scenic place blessed with it's natural beauty, is home to world famous Darjeeling tea and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway- A world heritage. Darjeeling also has the largest orchid species, for example Cypripedium, Dendrobidium, Cymbidium and so on. Although Darjeeling is a small city, it has the third tallest mountain in the world,Mount Kanchenjunga. it also has Tiger Hill so tall that you could get a glimpse over to Mount Everest. How to pronounce "Darjeeling".

History

The Gorkha War

The Gorkha War also known as Anglo Nepal War broke out in 1814 and ended 1816, due to a disagreement.The ruthles British declared war at the end of 1813. The gruesome war took place in Nepal. It was a war between the Kingdom of Nepal now called the Federal Democratic Republic Nepal, against British East Company. Fortunately for The British East Company, they had twenty-two thousand men as well as guns. However,The Kingdom of Nepal had less than twelve thousand men and not as many resources. The British had more cannons and guns,also a difference of six-thousand men.Lots of cannonballs were fired by the British. Consequently, because of all of the cannons,guns and men the British had, lots of the Nepalese army had been either injured or killed. As a result, the British won this horrific war and the war was over.

Geography

Darjeeling, is a small town situated in West Bengal, India. The development of this lovely town dated back to the mid nineteenth century. Darjeeling is a cold place, temperature in summer are surprisingly low at 15-20 degrees. Winters in Darjeling are very cold as well, they can go down to 0 degrees and below. During June-September there is very heavy rains, monsoon period also results in landslides. Darjeeling has quite a few hills and mountains, for instance, Tiger Hill and Mount Konchenjunga. So Darjeeling is quite a mountainous country as well as fairly dry. The climate is cool with occasionaly a heavy mist.
